About

Teaching That Builds
Real Understanding

Devendra Sir brings a focused, patient approach to CBSE Mathematics and Science that has helped hundreds of students across Classes 6–10 build genuine conceptual understanding — not just exam answers. Every student receives individual attention, and every concept is taught until it's truly clear.

Located in Kavi Nagar, Ghaziabad, the classes are available in-person, online, or as a flexible hybrid — designed to fit every family's schedule without compromising on quality. Doubt sessions, regular chapter tests, and ongoing progress feedback keep both students and parents fully informed throughout the year.

Our Method

How We Teach

A structured, step-by-step process designed to build genuine understanding and lasting results.

01

Concept Learning

Every new topic is introduced with clarity — the why behind every formula, not just the how.

02

Practice-Based Teaching

Structured problem sets progress from simple to complex — building speed, accuracy, and confidence together.

Common Questions

Which classes do you teach?

Devendra Sir teaches students from Class 6 through Class 10, covering the complete CBSE curriculum for Mathematics and Science in each class.

Do you teach CBSE students only?

The coaching is primarily designed for CBSE board students. The syllabus, question patterns, and exam preparation strategies are all CBSE-focused.

Are online classes available?

Yes — live online classes are available for all classes. Students get the same quality of teaching via video with digital notes, doubt support on WhatsApp, and access to recordings.

Are offline classes available?

Absolutely. In-person offline classes are held at CH/KD-16, Kavi Nagar, Ghaziabad. Students benefit from direct interaction, whiteboard teaching, and immediate doubt resolution in class.

What subjects do you teach?

Devendra Sir teaches Mathematics and Science for all classes from 6 to 10 — including all sub-topics of Physics, Chemistry, and Biology within Science.
